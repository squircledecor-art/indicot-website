02 Cast & Cure

Holographic brilliance — without the foil.

Cast & Cure presses a micro-embossed pattern into a UV-cured coating, then lifts the casting film away to be used again. What stays on the sheet is a brilliant holographic, micro-lens or high-gloss finish — no foil to buy, no laminate to strip.

01

Foil Saver™ economics

The casting film runs across thousands of impressions — cost drops far below cold or hot foil.

02

More sustainable

No foil waste stream, no laminate to strip — the printed sheet stays recyclable.

03

Effects on demand

Holographic rainbow, micro-lens, brushed metal and high-gloss — swap the film, change the look.
