B How it works

From plain sheet to finish.

Four stages — the Auto Screen With Industrial Foiler inline process in short.

01

Stop-cylinder feed

Sheets are gripped and registered on the stop-cylinder for offset-grade accuracy.

02

Screen coat

The rotary screen lays UV varnish or the cold-foil adhesive exactly where it's wanted.

03

Foil / hologram transfer

Cold foil or a holographic (Cast & Cure) film is bonded to the coated areas, then lifted away.

04

UV cure & deliver

Inline UV curing locks the finish; sheets stack automatically at delivery.
