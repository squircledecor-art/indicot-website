Lamination Machine

DUAL-SIDE

A dual-side thermal lamination machine for digital prints. A 2-roller system with mirror-chromed rollers gives high-gloss, feather-touch output; infrared + oil-based heating bonds even thicker films, with pneumatic pressure and a variable-speed drive for hassle-free long runs up to 3000 m.

B How it works

From plain sheet to finish.

Four stages — the DUAL-SIDE process in short.

01

Load film

Up to 3000 m film roll mounts on the bearing-shaft unwinder.

02

Heat

Infrared + oil-based rollers reach accurate, even temperature.

03

Bond

Pneumatic roller pressure bonds film for feather-touch gloss.

04

Output

DC motor + gearbox drive a steady, variable-speed long run.
