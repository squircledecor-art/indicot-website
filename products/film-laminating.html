B How it works

From printed sheet to finished stack.

Four stages — the automatic flying-knife process in short.

01

Auto feed

An offset feeder with pneumatic side-lay registers sheets at up to 15,000 per hour, with non-stop pre-stack feeding.

02

Coat & heat

Double chrome rolls meter glue at 2–3 g/m²; the electromagnetic + oil-heated drying roll dries it evenly at speed.

03

Laminate

A fully pneumatic pressure system bonds the film to the sheet with uniform, easily-adjusted pressure.

04

Flying knife & stack

The servo flying knife reads the overlap by ultrasonic probe and cuts clean; corrugated delivery stacks automatically.
