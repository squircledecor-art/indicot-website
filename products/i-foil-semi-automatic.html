i-Foil Semi-Automatic

IF-SA

The i-Foil is a foil-application system combining a flatbed screen printing machine, a hybrid UV-LED dryer and a foiling section. Foil adheres to cured UV glue for flat, tactile or 3D effects — plus Spot UV and Cast & Cure, with no dies and minimal foil waste. Built for a single operator with a servo auto take-off.
